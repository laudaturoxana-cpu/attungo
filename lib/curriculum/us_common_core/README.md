## Structure Notes
- Mathematics follows the CCSS-M domain structure: Operations & Algebraic Thinking, Number & Operations in Base Ten, Number & Operations — Fractions, Measurement & Data, Geometry (grade-dependent)
- English Language Arts follows CCSS-ELA: Reading Literature, Reading Informational Text, Writing, Speaking & Listening, Language
- Science topics follow NGSS (Next Generation Science Standards) which are commonly adopted alongside Common Core
- Social Studies standards are not part of CCSS but follow the C3 Framework (Council of Chief State School Officers)

## Adoption
Common Core has been adopted (fully or partially) by 41+ states as of 2024.
