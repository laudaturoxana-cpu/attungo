## About Core Knowledge Sequence
The Core Knowledge Sequence was developed by E.D. Hirsch Jr. and the Core Knowledge Foundation. It specifies a specific shared body of knowledge for each grade level (K-8), emphasising:
- **Content-rich curriculum**: Specific topics in history, geography, science, literature, and the arts
- **Building background knowledge**: Each grade builds on the previous, creating a "coherent, cumulative" curriculum
- **Cultural literacy**: Knowledge commonly shared in an educated American society

## Structure Notes
- Mathematics aligns closely with Common Core but is presented in a spiral, cumulative framework
- English Language Arts in Grades K-3 uses CKLA (Core Knowledge Language Arts), emphasising phonics and content-rich read-alouds
- History is organised as World History + American History with specific topics designated per grade
- Science follows a specific sequence of topics per grade, not a spiral

## Adoption
Core Knowledge is used in approximately 1,000+ schools across the US and widely by homeschool families.
